  ABOUT US

One major challenge lottery players in Nigeria face is the difficulty in forecasting the winning numbers to be drawn by lottery machines and this has made a lot of players unhappy over the years.

In a bid to change this narrative, put smiles on the faces of lottery players, and give everyone an equal chance of winning without stress, Simple Touch Applications Limited launches Simple Lotto!

Simple Lotto is an interesting, bi-gender friendly lottery model that gives you a chance to win big with small stakes, without worrying about numbers! All that is needed to play is a phone number!

When you enter your phone number and click PLAY, a UNIQUE TICKET ID will be generated for you. This unique ticket ID qualifies you for the stated winning -that's all!

When that game closes by 6pm, all Ticket IDs generated are shuffled by our bespoke lottery machine and winning Ticket IDs are selected and published on our website under "WINNERS".

When you win, your account is credited instantly! Simple, right? We know!

  BONUSES

You get 100% Welcome Bonus on your first deposit and 20% Bonus on subsequent deposits of ₦ 1,000 and above. Anytime you get a bonus on your deposit, both the main amount deposited and the bonus added would be merged together in your bonus account.

Funds/stakes will be deducted from your bonus account first, before your main account except when there are no funds in the bonus account. Every time you win, your main account will be credited with your winnings instantly.
