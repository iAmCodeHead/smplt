HOW TO PLAY

    To participate in a draw, enter your mobile number under the draw category and click on PLAY!

    The predetermined amount displayed will be deducted from your account and a UNIQUE ticket number will be generated for you. This ticket number qualifies you for the stated draw.

    You're advised to play multiple times to increase your chance. Everytime you enter your mobile number, a DIFFERENT ticket number is generated and multiple ticket numbers increases your chances of winning !

    For every draw, our bespoke software shuffles all tickets numbers entered for the draw and picks the winning tickets.

    The winners' account are automatically credited after each draw and instant withdrawals can be made by winner. Simple, isn't it?
